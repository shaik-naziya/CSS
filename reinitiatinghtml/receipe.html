How to make Blueberry Ice-Cream

Ingredients
  • 1 cup full fat coconut milk(from a can)
  • 1/2 cup sweetened condensed coconut milk.
  • 1-1/2 cup blueberries ( freh and frozen)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract pinch salt

This is an overview of the ingredients. 

  • Blueberries: You can use fresh or frozen blueberries.
  • Sweetened condensed coconut milk: Adds a rich sweet creamy flavor. You could also use sweetened condensed oat milk.
  • Coconut milk: I used full-fat coconut milk. It adds a rich creamy flavor.
  • Vanilla extract: Enhances flavors and adds flavor.
  • Salt: Enhances sweetness.

Instructions

Step:1 Add ingredients to a high speed blender.

step:2 Mix until smooth and creamy. Pour into a freezer safe and container and freeze.

step:3 Remove from freezer and the ice cream is ready to serve.



Notes:

More blueberries give a stronger blueberry flavor, but also increase an icy texture because of the water content. Taste as you go to see how you like it. Nutritional info is for 1 cup of blueberries. This recipe will last up to a month in an airtight container in the freezer.
