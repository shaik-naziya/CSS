BLUEBERRY ICE-CREAM

Description of the reciepe

This blueberry ice cream will add a sweet ending to any meal. It's best to make the base and chill it overnight before processing in your ice cream maker. Serve topped with fresh strawberries or raspberries.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 Cups heavy cream
  • 2 Packages (6 ounces) Driscoll's Blueberries
  • 1 Cup sugar
  • 1/8 Tsp. salt
  • 1 Cup whole milk
  • 1 Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
      
     

Preparation Steps Of Blueberry-ice-cream

  1. COMBINE blueberries, sugar, and salt in a heavy saucepan.
  2. HEAT blueberry mixture over medium-high heat until sugar dissolves while STIRRING occasionally.
  3. CONTINUE HEATING blueberry mixture until it comes to a boil.
  4. REDUCE heat.
  5. SIMMER blueberry mixture for an additional 10 minutes.
  6. REMOVE blueberry mixture from heat.
  7. COOL for 20 minutes.
  8. URÉE blueberry mixture in a blender.
  9. COMBINE blueberry mixture, heavy cream, milk, and lemon juice in a large bowl.
  10. COVER ice cream base with plastic wrap.
  11. CHILL ice cream base in refrigerator overnight.
  12. POUR ice cream base into an ice cream maker.
  13. PROCESS ice cream according to manufacturer's directions.




FALUDA


Description Of Faluda

Falooda is a delicious, refreshing, cooling, summer time dessert that you can sip on and enjoy with a spoon too. This exotic dessert is made using jelly, cooked thin vermicelli, sabja seeds,(basil seeds), milk, syrup, and ice cream. It is the Indian Sub Continent’s version of a sundae. The concoction is a burst of different textures and flavors.

Ingrediants Need For Making Faluda:

  • Milk – chilled dairy milk is preferred. However, you can always try a nut milk.
  • Vermicelli – known as sev, some like to prepare cornflour one.
  • Sabja Seeds – also known as basil seeds or takmariya.
  • Jelly – these days vegetarian jelly is easily available.
  • Rose Syrup – use good quality rose syrup.
  • Ice Cream – can use vanilla or strawberry ice cream.
  • Nuts – chopped nuts to garnish. Use any of your choice.

      
     

Preparation of faluda

  1. As per the instructions on the packet prepare the jelly.
  2. Soak the falooda seeds in 1 cup of water and leave till required.
  3. Boil about 2 cups of water. Add the thin vermicelli to it and boil for 3 to 5 minutes
  4. If you are using pre roasted vermicelli then you need to soak it in water for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Drain out into a sieve. Wash under cold water. Leave it in the sieve till required.
  6. To make the falooda, take 4 tall glasses. Put in each glass a tablespoon of rose syrup.
  7. Cut the jelly into tiny cubes. Add some in all 4 glasses.
  8. Drain out the falooda seeds into a sieve. Spoon the seeds equally into the 4 glasses.
  9. At this stage if the vermicelli is sticking, just run it under cold water.
  10. Add ½ cup chilled milk into each glass.
  11. Add a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  12. Top with the chopped nuts.
  13. Serve each glass with a straw and spoon.



CHOCLATE CUP CAKE


Description

Chocolate cupcakes are the ultimate party food. These are super moist and bursting with rich, chocolaty flavor — and they're so easy to make. Frost these delicious chocolate cupcakes with buttercream or cream cheese frosting, or try royal icing or even whipped cream.

      
     

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon baking soda
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • ¾ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup milk

Preparation Of CupCakes

  1. STEP:1Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a muffin pan with paper or foil liners.
  2. STEP:2Sift together flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt
  3. STEP:3Cream together sugar and butter in a large bowl until light and fluffy.
  4. STEP:4Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla. Add flour mixture in two batches, alternating with milk; beat well.
  5. STEP:5Spoon batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling each 3/4 full.
  6. STEP:6Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the centers comes out clean, 15 to 17 minutes. Remove from the oven and let cool before serving or frosting.
